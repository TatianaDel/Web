About partnerships

Partner companies of the formation :
The IT departments of the IUTs have large IT companies as partners. This allows students to integrate these companies more easily at the end of their formation, via the end-of-year internship.

Voici une liste non-exhaustive des sociétés partenaires:
Atos Origin | Bull | Capgemini | Cigref | IBM | Microsoft | Syntec | Unilog

International partnership :
In addition to professional partnerships, the IT departments in partnership with foreign universities welcome foreign students to train them in IT. But also by sending students to do their internship abroad.
