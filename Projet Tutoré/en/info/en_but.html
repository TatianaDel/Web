Informations about the DUT Informatique

  1. The IT DUT
  2. Reform : BUT

Following the reform of the baccalaureate for the year 2020, the IUT of France also looked into a reform to allow future generations to pursue, if they wish, a DUT. It will be replaced from the start of the 2021 school year by a Technological University Bachelor (BUT)
This new diploma is a fusion of a DUT in 2 years and a professional license of one year, they will then be higher studies over 3 years, they will have the degrees of license.
The BUT is a bit like the old DUT, the 24 specialties, including computer science in particular, become “mentions” and the options of “courses”, they simply change their name so that the next BUT students can keep their landmarks.

About the news :

  1. Only 3 mentions in the first year : Social careers, Biological engineering and Information - communication. They will be chosen when registering on Parcoursup.
  2. A wish for a course on Parcoursup will count as a wish and no longer a sub-wish.

It is good to know that not all IUTs will necessarily offer all the courses that a mention offers, and that the selection criteria for GOALS are not so different from those for DUTs.
