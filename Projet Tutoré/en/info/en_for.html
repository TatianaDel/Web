Spread across France, 115 University Institutes of Technology ensure strong anchoring in the local and regional economic environment.

The IUT has a university teaching quality recognized and acclaimed by companies for more than 40 years, thanks to the establishment of programs adapted to professional reality and the creation of diplomas responding to the emergence of new professions.

Formations is the strength and success of IUT. They cover most of the secondary and tertiary sectors, and offer the collaboration of professionals. This allows our students to find a job within 6 months following graduation.

This diversification of diplomas and training leads to the implementation of different teaching methods, which thus suit a greater number of students and professionals wishing to resume studies or to carry out a validation of acquired experience (VAE).

For IUTs in partnership with foreign universities, further study abroad and internships are offered.
