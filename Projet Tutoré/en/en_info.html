Informations about the DUT Informatique

  1. The IT DUT
  2. Reform : BUT

The IT departments of the IUT of France train students in IT and teamwork in order to integrate them into professional life

They therefore offer training on a human scale, supervised by available teachers, as well as by experienced professionals. which provide students with practical and concrete experience in IT professions

Computer science education (DUT or Professional License) is based on 3 axes, each of them requiring specific qualities and skills:

Analysis and design :
Preliminary work in relation with the client to specify his needs and expectations and the design of the diagram of a computer processing.
Communication, openness, analytical and synthesis skills ...

Algorithmic and programming :
Effective implementation of processing up to coding.
Logical mind, technical precision ...

System architecture and networks :
Operation of a computer, its operating system and networks (Internet, Intranet, ...).
Curiosity, patience ...
